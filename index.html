The Campus Has Eyes

Mia Corcoran is studying photography at Blackwood University. She’s looking for art. Instead, she finds a pattern.

A blurred figure in the background of a photo taken yesterday. The same figure in an archive scan from 2003. The same figure in a yearbook from 1995. He hasn't aged a day.

As Mia hunts for this "ghost," the vent in her dorm room begins to rattle. Items go missing. And she realizes: He isn't haunting the campus. He’s living in it.
